Shediastis is free for YOU

Shediastis is a free to use Daily Life Planner and Organizing application. You can use Shediastis to make your daily life more productive by planning ahed and mapping your day to day activities.

Get Started
Features

Here are some of the features you can use.

  • Managing Events

    You can add certain events to your schedule and mark them as completed or keep them in the trash or completely delete them.

  • Fitness Organizer

    You can create exercise routines and add already existing exircises to them or create and add your own custom exercises.

  • Reminders

    You can set reminders that can be reused and can be set to repeat after certain time spans.
